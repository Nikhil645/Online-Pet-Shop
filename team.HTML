OUR TEAM

Piyush kadam

Piyush Kadam is an Indian wildlife expert famously known for his television show "The Crocodile Hunter." Born in Indiass, as a boy he helped his parents with care and feeding of animals at their reptile and fauna park. He was given a large python on his 6th birthday and was wrestling crocodiles by age 9. He died when a stingray pierced him in the chest while filming "Ocean's Deadliest."

Rohan Kadam

Sir Rohan Kadam is a world-famous wildlife documentarian. He has made educational and entertaining wildlife films for 10 years and produced some of the first black-and-white nature films. A household name, he is one of Britain's most prominent broadcasters. His latest series is called "Life of Birds."

Nikhil Ingale

nikhil ingale is a famous ocean explorer who pioneered underwater documentaries. His team created the first underwater base camp so that researchers could stay underwater for weeks at a time -- a technology that was adopted later by the United States Navy. He started off as an explorer and spear fisherman but later became an environmentalist that fought to restrict commercial whaling and underwater nuclear waste dumping.
